DuckDuckGo default themes support

DuckDuckGo search engine comes with 4 search themes: Default, Basic, Gray and Black. Our app supports them natively to ensure a more unified search experience. More themes are coming and we plan to make it more customizable in the future so stay tuned for that !

Local search history

All your search queries are stored locally in your phone storage. We do not share any information about your search activity on the Internet.

Improved DuckDuckGo mobile search experience

DuckDuckGo Search & Widget provides an enhanced experience of search combining between DuckDuckGo privacy respct and near-Google app experience. With this app, you can make your search queries on DuckDuckGo faster and more efficient than ever.
